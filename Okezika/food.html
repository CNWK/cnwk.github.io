Pizza

Pizza is a very popular food. You can add different toppings to customize pizza to your preference. Some people choose not to add toppings and eat a normal cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Form the dough into a circle.
  2. Spread the sauce and cheese on the dough.
  3. Add pepperoni.
  4. Bake in an oven.
