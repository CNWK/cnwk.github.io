Ingerdients

  • Refried Taco Shells
  • Mexican Salsa (mild works best with families)
  • Refried Beans
  • Shreeded Cheese (prefferably Mixed varieties)
  • Lettuce (well cut)
  • Tomatoes (cut)
  • Cilantro (optional)

Steps to Make

  1. Preheat oven at 200 degrees celcius
  2. Put Taco Shells in oven
  3. Saute Refried beans
  4. Spread Refried Beans on Taco Shells
  5. Spread Salsa on top
  6. Cut Tomatoes and Lettuce
  7. Drizzle Tomatoes, Lettuce, and Cilantro on Taco
  8. Serve Taco Immediatly
