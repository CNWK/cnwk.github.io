My favorite food

PIZZA

Ingredients
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Bread
  • Pepperoni
Instructions
  1. Roll the dough
  2. Put the tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Put the cheese on top of the sauce
  4. Put the pizza in the oven for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
