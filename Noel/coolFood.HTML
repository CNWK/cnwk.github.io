Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • dough
  • marinara sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. flatten dough
  2. spread marinara sauce on dough
  3. sprinkle mozzarella cheese on
  4. sprinkle parmesan cheese
  5. bake for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Eat. (Yes, eating is a step. A very hard step at that. Have you ever eaten a entire pizza by yourself. What is this 'share with others' you speak of?)
