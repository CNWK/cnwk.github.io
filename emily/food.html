Recipe for Pizza

Ingredients

  • dough
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 500 degrees
  2. first flatten the dough
  3. Next spread you tomato sauce on the dough
  4. Then put cheese on top of the tomato sauce
  5. last put it in the oven unless you want to add toppings like pepperoni
