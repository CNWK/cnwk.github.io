ingreideints

  • tortia
  • chicken
  • bean rice
  • lettuse and cheese
  • (optional) creamy jalipano
  • unions and peppers

  2. step 2 add the chicken in the middle of your tortia
  3. step 3 add the beans and rice on the chicken
  4. step 4 add the lettuse and cheese on the beans and rice
  5. step 5 add the creamy jalipano on the lettuse and cheese
  6. step 6 add the unions and the peppers on the creamy jalipano
