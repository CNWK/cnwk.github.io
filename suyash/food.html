My favorite food is tacos,lets make one!!!

a recipe to make one

ingredients

  1. pita bread
  2. cheese
  3. guacamole
  4. tomatoes
  5. OPTIONAL:any sauce you want

intructions

  1. spread the guacamole on the pita bread
  2. put the cheese on the guacamole
  3. drizzle the tomatoes on the cheese
  4. put the sauce on the cheese too
  5. roll the pita bread
  6. cook the roll on a pan and your done!
