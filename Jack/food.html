    How to Make Pokeball Rice Cereal Treats
  1. Start by lining a baking sheet with wax paper and set it aside. Then, In one medium-sized saucepan, melt 1 tbsp butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the butter is melted pour 1 cup of mini marshmallows into the buttered saucepan and stir until melted and remove the saucepan from heat.
  3. Next, fold in 1 ½ cups rice cereal and stir until all ingredients are well combined.
  4. Roll mixture into 2-inch spheres – you will need 5 of them. Place gently on the lined baking sheet.
  5. Next, In a clean, medium-sized saucepan repeat the same steps with your remaining butter, marshmallows.
  6. Once you remove the saucepan from the heat, add red gel food coloring and mix until completely tinted and fold in remaining rice cereal.
