One of My Favorite Foods

I have a lot of favorite foods. One of them is spaghetti and meatballs.

Spaghetti is good. It from Italy.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Beef
  • Pasta
  • Spices (Salt, Pepper, Oregano, Italian Spices
  1. Boil water and cook your pasta according to the package directions.
  2. Mix your beef and spices together and roll it into a ball.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Cook the meatballs in the oven for 20 minutes.
  4. Put the tomatoes and more spices into a pot and cook.
  5. Plate everything and enjoy!
